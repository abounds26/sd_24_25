Allison Rogers 8/21/24

1.1 Providing text alternatives helps people translate it into other forms such as larger prints, braille, speech, symbols or simpler language. It is more important to have non-text information available to more people

1.2 Audio and video content can be understood by more people. Videos can be played with captions for those that are hard of hearing.

1.3 Accurate and understandable content structure is very important because it allows the website's words or orientation to be changed with out its purpose being changed.

1.4 Color is one of many ways people distinguish information. If you have audio allow people to turn it down or mute it. If you have a video make sure the background sound doesn't interrupt. Allowing users to change how text looks makes it easier to be seen by them.
